“Difference equations are the memory of a system about its own past.”
— Unknown mathematician
Difference equations describe how the value of a variable changes over time based on its previous values. In this way, the system "remembers" its history, making these equations a natural tool for modeling dynamic processes in economics, biology, and physics.

“Diferenčné rovnice sú pamäťou systému o jeho vlastnej minulosti.”
— Neznámy matematik
